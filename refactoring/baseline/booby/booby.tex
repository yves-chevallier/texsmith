\section{Introduction}\label{introduction}

Boobies are seabirds in the genus \emph{Sula}, family Sulidae. They are
large, long-winged birds that plunge-dive for fish. The name \enquote{booby}
originates from the Spanish word \enquote{bobo}, meaning \enquote{stupid} or \enquote{clown},
due to the birds' apparent lack of fear of humans.

\section{Particularities}\label{particularities}

Boobies have several distinctive features:

\begin{itemize}
\item{} They have brightly colored feet, which they use in mating displays.
\item{} They are known for their spectacular diving ability, plunging into
  the water from great heights to catch fish.

\end{itemize}
